Inquisitory Manifesto

What Is Art?

What is art? How do we set the boundaries? How do we discern what is or isn't?

How do we decide what is more or less deserving of that title? Who does the deciding?

When did we start letting people tell us that our art is less simply because it's not "traditional" or "hard" enough?

When do we stop putting one form above another?

When do we learn that art is simply what we make it?
